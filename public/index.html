Products We Use

We carefully select the highest quality skincare products available in the spa industry.

PCA Skin

The originator of advanced blended chemical peels. PCA SKIN® has been and continues to be a trusted innovator in the development of highly effective professional treatments and daily care products.

Le Mieux

This skincare line has garnered the reputation of "The Serum Authority" for its diverse selection of scientifically advanced serums with three-tier delivery systems, timed-release technology, and concentrated ingredients from premium sources.

Eminence Organic SkinCare

100% Certified Organic and all natural skincare handcrafted in Hungary. Eminence uses only the finest and freshest fruit pulps, seeds, exotic herbs, essential oils, plant extract and vegetables in their products.

Skin Script RX

Natural, yet effective skin care designed for anti-aging, hyperpigmentation, sun damage, acne and rosacea. Fruit-based antioxidants, exfoliants, and natural brighteners are the base of this skin care. Ideal for treating all skin types and ethnicities. All products are paraben free and no animal testing.

NuFACE

Non-invasive award-winning anti-aging device; clinically proven for improved facial contour, skin tone and wrinkle reduction in as little as 5 minutes a day.

PFB Vanish

PFB vanish is a gentle, cosmetically elegant roll-on gel formulated to prevent and treat ingrown hairs, razor burn and bumps resulting from hair removal.

Sun Laboratories

Established in California, Sun Labs is leading the industry with professional sunless tanning solutions, aloe based and paraben free formula.

Grande Lash MD

Be LashRAGEOUS with GrandeLASH-MD, a 5x award winning eyelash conditioner, created with a proprietary blend of vitamins, peptides, amino acids, plus conditioning ingredients.
